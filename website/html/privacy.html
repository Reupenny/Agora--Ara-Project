Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

At Agora, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our marketplace platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name (first and last name)
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Account type (Buyer, Seller, or Agora Admin)
  • Password (stored securely with encryption)

Business Information (For Sellers)

  • Business name
  • Business location
  • Business details and description
  • Product information and images

Automatically Collected Information

  • Session data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send administrative information and notifications
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Communicate updates about your products or orders

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Buyers: Sellers' business information and product details are visible to all users
  • Within Your Business: Business administrators can view team member information
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing with salt
  • Secure database connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@agora.co.nz

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login state and improve your experience. These cookies:

  • Are essential for platform functionality
  • Expire when you close your browser or log out
  • Do not track you across other websites

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain certain information for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution
  • Fraud prevention
  • Audit purposes

8. Children's Privacy

Agora is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes
  • Displaying a notice on the platform

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@agora.co.nz
  • Phone: +64 3 123 4567
  • Address: Agora Marketplace Ltd., 123 Commerce Street, Christchurch 8011, New Zealand

11. New Zealand Privacy Act Compliance

Agora complies with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. We are committed to protecting your privacy rights under New Zealand law.
