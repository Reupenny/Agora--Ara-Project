Welcome to Agora

Agora is New Zealand's dedicated online marketplace connecting businesses and passionate sellers with customers who appreciate quality, history, and style. Forget the clutter of generic retail; we focus on the items that tell a story. Whether you're a gallery showcasing original Abstract Oil Paintings, a retailer curating Mid-Century Teak Furniture, or a small nursery offering that elusive Variegated Monstera, Agora provides the platform to reach your perfect buyer. With a clear separation between buyer and seller accounts and a robust system for businesses to manage their sales teams, we make trading beautiful things simple and secure.
