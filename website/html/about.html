About Agora

Agora is New Zealand's dedicated online marketplace connecting businesses and passionate sellers with customers who appreciate quality, history, and style.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Agora was created to address a gap in the New Zealand market. We saw talented retailers, galleries, and artisans struggling to reach their ideal customers through generic marketplaces that didn't understand their unique products or values.

Forget the clutter of generic retail; we focus on the items that tell a story. Whether you're a gallery showcasing original artwork, a retailer curating mid-century furniture, or a small nursery offering rare plants, Agora provides the platform to reach your perfect buyer.

What Makes Us Different

  • Curated Quality: We focus on homewares, plants, and unique finds that have character and story
  • Business-Friendly: Robust tools for businesses to manage their sales teams and product catalog
  • Clear Roles: Separate buyer and seller accounts ensure a professional marketplace experience
  • NZ Focused: Built for New Zealand businesses and buyers, with local support and understanding
  • Secure & Simple: Easy-to-use platform with secure transactions and reliable customer service

For Sellers

Agora empowers businesses of all sizes to reach customers who truly appreciate quality products. Our platform includes:

  • Business profile pages to showcase your brand story
  • Team management tools for businesses with multiple sellers
  • Product catalog management with tags and categories
  • Order tracking and customer communication
  • Analytics to understand your customers and sales
